How to add VSO to Metamask

  1. First, make sure your Metamask is connected with Avalanche Network. If you don’t see Avalache on the list of options, click here

  2. Once you’ve connected to Avalache, choose your account and click “Add Token”. Enter the VSO contract address found here.
